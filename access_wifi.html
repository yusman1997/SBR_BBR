How do I connect my device to the Access Wifi?

Windows

  1. Open the Wi-Fi settings on your device.
  2. Click Icon >
  3. select Binus-Guest, Click Connect
  4. Input Password:Please scan the barcode on the desk, NEXT
  5. Wait until the device is successfully connected to the Wi-Fi network.

macOS

  1. Open the Wi-Fi settings on your device, select Binus-Guest
  2. Click Join/Connect
  3. Password:Please scan the barcode on the desk
  4. Click Join.
  5. Proceed with any certificate authentication prompts.

iOS

  1. Open the Wi-Fi settings on your device, select Binus-Guest
  2. Click Join/Connect
  3. Password:Please scan the barcode on the desk
  4. Click Join.
  5. Proceed with any certificate authentication prompts.
