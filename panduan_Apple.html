How do I connect to a meeting room TV?

You can connect to the meeting room TV via AirPlay

What do I need to check first?

  • Ensure the TV is powered on.
  • Connect to one of the following Wi-Fi networks:
    • Access Wifi
  • Ensure you disconnect any VPNs.

The device is already connected to the Wi-Fi network. Please make sure it is not connected to a VPN, as it will not be able to connect to the meeting room TV.

Connecting to the TV in a meeting room AirPlay Via MacBook

1. Click Control Center in the top-right corner.

2. Select Screen Mirroring.

3. Choose the device named TV (SBR-SmartTV).

4. Enter the AirPlay code for verification; the passcode will appear on the TV screen.

5. Your screen will be displayed on the TV.

Connecting to the TV in a meeting room AirPlay Via iPad Or iPhone

1. Swipe down from the top-right corner of the screen to access Control Center.

2. Select Screen Mirroring.

3. Choose the device named TV (SBR-SmartTV).

4. Enter the AirPlay code for verification; the passcode will appear on the TV screen.

5. Your screen will be displayed on the TV.
